# LinkedIn Engagement & Growth Strategy: Algorithmic Trading

## 1. Automated Discovery & Data Collection
- [ ] **Target Identification**:
    - [ ] Search for "Algorithmic Trading", "Quant Finance", "HFT", and "Systematic Trading".
    - [ ] Use LlamaIndex to filter posts that have high engagement (>20 comments).
- [ ] **Contact Extraction**:
    - [ ] Extract authors of trending articles and save to `influencers.csv`.
    - [ ] Extract active commenters (the "engaged audience") and save to `audience.csv`.

## 2. Content Optimization (Improving Your Articles)
- [ ] **Hook Transformation**: 
    - [ ] Re-write article openings to be "Scroll-Stoppers" (Start with a shocking stat or a common trading failure).
    - [ ] *Example*: "Why 90% of Algorithmic Traders fail in the first 6 months (and it's not the code)."
- [ ] **Formatting for Readability**:
    - [ ] Use bold headers and bullet points.
    - [ ] Keep paragraphs under 3 lines for mobile users.
- [ ] **Strategic Tagging**:
    - [ ] Tag 2-3 relevant experts mentioned in your research in the *comments* (not the post body) to ask for their opinion.

## 3. The "90-Minute" Engagement Hack
- [ ] **Initial Boost**:
    - [ ] Immediately after posting, add the first comment yourself with a "Bonus Tip" or a question to kickstart the algorithm.
    - [ ] Reply to EVERY comment within the first 90 minutes to signal "Quality" to the LinkedIn algorithm.
- [ ] **Engagement Sequencing**:
    - [ ] Strategy: Comment first (at least 5 words), THEN like. This carries more weight in 2025.

## 4. Audience Outreach & Conversion
- [ ] **Personalized Connection Requests**:
    - [ ] Reach out to the `audience.csv` list.
    - [ ] *Template*: "Hey [Name], saw your insightful comment on [Influencer]'s post about Mean Reversion. I just wrote a piece on how to automate that specific strategy—would love your thoughts."
- [ ] **Direct Value Add**:
    - [ ] Send a "No-Strings-Attached" PDF or code snippet related to your article to high-value targets.

## 5. Iterative Refinement
- [ ] **Analytics Review**: Check which "Hooks" got the most 'See More' clicks.
- [ ] **A/B Testing**: Try posting at 9:00 AM vs 1:00 PM (Tuesday-Thursday).
