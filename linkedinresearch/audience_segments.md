# Algorithmic Trading: Audience Segments & Needs

To get engagement, we must stop writing "about" algorithmic trading and start writing "for" these specific people. Here is the audience breakdown for 2025:

## 1. The "Aspiring Alpha" (Retail & Junior Quants)
*   **Who they are**: CS/Math students, retail traders trying to automate, or junior devs at non-trading firms.
*   **What they search for**: "How to start", "Python for finance", "Backtesting libraries", "Free data sources".
*   **Their Pain Point**: **Analysis Paralysis.** They have the code but don't know if their strategy is "real" or just overfitted noise.
*   **Engagement Hook**: "The 3 backtesting traps that make every strategy look profitable (until you trade live)."

## 2. The "Risk-Averse Manager" (Portfolio Managers & CIOs)
*   **Who they are**: Decision-makers at small/mid-sized funds who oversee quants but aren't coding daily.
*   **What they search for**: "Risk management frameworks", "Drawdown recovery", "Market regime detection", "Compliance in Algos".
*   **Their Pain Point**: **Black Box Fear.** They need to explain *why* a model lost money to their investors.
*   **Engagement Hook**: "How to explain a 15% drawdown to your LPs without saying 'the model broke'."

## 3. The "Infrastructure Architect" (HFT/System Devs)
*   **Who they are**: Low-latency engineers, C++ experts, and cloud architects building the pipes.
*   **What they search for**: "FPGA vs GPU", "Linux kernel tuning for trading", "Fix protocol optimization", "Zero-copy networking".
*   **Their Pain Point**: **Slippage & Latency.** Every millisecond is lost revenue.
*   **Engagement Hook**: "Why your Python execution is costing you 40 bps in slippage (and how to bridge to C++)."

## 4. The "Skeptical Discretionary Trader"
*   **Who they are**: Manual traders who are "quant-curious" but think algos are "stealing their edge."
*   **What they search for**: "Order flow trading", "Institutional footprints", "Can AI beat the market?".
*   **Their Pain Point**: **FOMO.** They see the world moving to automation and feel left behind.
*   **Engagement Hook**: "3 things a human trader sees that an algorithm will always miss (and how to automate the rest)."
