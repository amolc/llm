# Targeting Strategy: Prop Traders

Prop Traders are an ideal audience for our **Intelligence Platform** and **Auto Trading Platform**. They manage significant capital but are often limited by firm-imposed rules, manual execution errors, and "black-box" risk monitoring.

## 1. The Prop Trader Persona
*   **The "Challenge Chaser"**: Retail traders trying to pass evaluations (FTMO, Topstep, etc.).
    *   **Pain Point**: Failing due to strict drawdown rules or lack of disciplined risk management.
*   **The "Funded Pro"**: Experienced traders who have already secured capital.
    *   **Pain Point**: Scaling their strategy without increasing screen time or making manual errors.
*   **The "Quant-Prop"**: Traders at institutional prop firms (Jane Street, Hudson River Trading).
    *   **Pain Point**: Accessing sophisticated data analysis tools for their personal alpha research.
