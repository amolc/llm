# Lead Generation Strategy: Portfolio Automation & Analysis Tools

To get customers for your portfolio tools, you must move from "selling a product" to "solving a risk." This strategy focuses on finding high-intent leads and converting them through authority.

## 1. Target Customer Profiles (The "Who")
*   **Active Retail Traders**: Managing $50k+ and struggling with emotional discipline or manual errors.
*   **Small Fund Managers / Family Offices**: Overseeing $1M-$10M who need to professionalize their reporting and risk management.
- **High-Net-Worth Individuals (HNWIs)**: Sophisticated investors who want "Quant-level" insights on their personal holdings.

## 2. Discovery: Finding the Leads
Use these "Intent Signals" on LinkedIn to find people already looking for solutions:
*   **Signal A: The "Complainer"**: Look for people commenting on posts about "market volatility," "getting stopped out," or "unexpected drawdowns."
*   **Signal B: The "Competitor's Audience"**: Go to the LinkedIn pages of tools like *Portfolio Visualizer*, *Koyfin*, or *QuantConnect*. Look at who is asking "how-to" questions in their comments.
*   **Signal C: Group Mining**: Join groups like "Quantitative Finance Professionals" or "Algorithmic Trading Wealth" and filter members by "Seniority: Senior/Director."

## 3. Conversion: The Outreach Framework
Stop sending generic "Hi, I have a tool" messages. Use the **"Insight-First"** approach:

### **Template: The "Gap Analysis" Request**
> "Hi [Name], I saw your comment on [Influencer]'s post about the recent tech sector drawdown. 
> 
> I've been analyzing how automated risk-parity models handled that specific move compared to manual portfolios. I noticed a 4% 'gap' in recovery time that most manual traders miss. 
> 
> I built a small analysis tool to visualize this—would you be open to me running your top 3 tickers through it just to show you the data? No pitch, just thought you'd find the delta interesting."

## 4. Content Funnel: The "Magnet"
Create 3 specific types of posts to attract these customers:
1.  **The "Before & After"**: Show a chart of a manual portfolio vs. an automated one during a crash (e.g., Aug 2024 carry trade unwind).
2.  **The "Feature Tease"**: A 30-second video of your tool's dashboard specifically showing a **Risk Heatmap** or **Slippage Calculator**.
3.  **The "Expert Opinion"**: "Why 2025 is the year manual portfolio management becomes a liability."

## 5. Automation Workflow
- **Step 1**: Use a tool like *Sales Navigator* to save a list of "Fund Managers" who have posted in the last 30 days.
- **Step 2**: Use *LlamaIndex* (via our script) to extract their specific pain points from their posts.
- **Step 3**: Send 10 hyper-personalized messages per day using the template above.
