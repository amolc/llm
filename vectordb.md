This module explores the core technologies that enable **Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)**. We move from simple keyword search to high-dimensional semantic retrieval, focusing on Vector DBs, Chunking, and Data Security.

## **Quick Summary**

- **Embeddings**: Numerical representations of text that capture meaning.
- **Vector DBs**: Specialized databases for searching millions of vectors in milliseconds.
- **RAG Foundation**: Effective chunking and high-quality embeddings are the first steps to a successful RAG system.
- **Security**: Always mask PII before embedding to protect sensitive user data.
